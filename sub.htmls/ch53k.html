OVERVIEW

The CH-53K is a heavy-lift transport helicopter. It is designed for extreme cargo and troop transport missions. It serves mainly with the United States Marine Corps. The helicopter can carry very heavy military equipment. It is built for ship-based and land operations. Its design focuses on power and efficiency. It is the most capable CH-53 variant.

DEVELOPMENT HISTORY

The CH-53K was developed to replace older CH-53E models. Development began in the early 2000s. The first flight took place in 2015. It was designed by Sikorsky Aircraft. Engineers focused on increasing lifting capacity. Advanced materials and engines were introduced. It entered service in the late 2010s.

The J-10C is an upgraded version of the J-10 fighter family. The letter C represents a more advanced and modern variant. It symbolizes China’s growing air power capability. The aircraft is a key part of modern Chinese air force strength.

The J-10C is a fourth-generation plus multirole fighter aircraft. It is designed for both air-to-air and air-to-ground missions. The fighter is widely used by the Chinese Air Force. The J-10C is an important part of China’s air defense system.

The J-10 was developed by Chengdu Aircraft Corporation in China. Development began in the 1980s and continued for many years. The J-10C is the latest upgraded variant of the series. The program marked a major milestone in Chinese aviation industry.

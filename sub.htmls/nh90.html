The NH90 name stands for NATO Helicopter 90. It was developed as a modern multi-role NATO project. The number 90 refers to the 1990s development era. It was designed for European military cooperation. The name reflects its international origin. It represents a new generation of military helicopters. Today it is used by many NATO countries.

OVERVIEW

The NH90 is a medium multi-role military helicopter. It is designed for transport and naval missions. The helicopter supports troop and cargo movement. It can also perform search and rescue operations. It is built for modern battlefield requirements. The aircraft is widely used across Europe. It is one of the most advanced NATO helicopters.

The NH90 was developed by a European consortium. Development began in the late 1980s. The first flight took place in 1995. It was created to replace older military helicopters. Multiple countries contributed to its design. It entered service in the 2000s. It became a major European defense project.

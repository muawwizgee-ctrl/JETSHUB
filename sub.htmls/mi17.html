DESCRIPTION

ABOUT ITS NAME

The Mi-17 is a military transport helicopter developed in Russia. NATO assigned it the reporting name "Hip." The helicopter is an upgraded version of the famous Mi-8. Its name is recognized around the world. The aircraft has become one of the most successful helicopters ever built. It is known for reliability and versatility. Today the Mi-17 serves in many military and civilian organizations.

OVERVIEW

The Mi-17 is a medium twin-engine transport helicopter. It is designed to carry troops, cargo, and equipment. The helicopter can perform transport and rescue missions. Its large cabin allows flexible mission configurations. The aircraft is widely used by military forces worldwide. It performs effectively in difficult environments. The Mi-17 remains one of the most popular helicopters in service.

DEVELOPMENT HISTORY

The Mi-17 was developed from the Mi-8 helicopter family. Development focused on improving performance and reliability. The helicopter entered service during the late twentieth century. Engineers introduced more powerful engines and upgrades. The aircraft quickly gained international popularity. Many variants were developed for different missions. The Mi-17 became a major success in global aviation.

DESIGN FEATURES

The Mi-17 features a spacious cabin for troops and cargo. Large rear doors allow easy loading and unloading. The helicopter uses a five-blade main rotor system. Its rugged construction supports operations in harsh conditions. The aircraft can be fitted with mission-specific equipment. A strong landing gear improves performance on rough terrain. The design emphasizes reliability and versatility.

PERFORMANCE

The Mi-17 can reach speeds of approximately 250 km/h. It performs well in mountainous and hot environments. The helicopter has excellent lifting capability. Its twin engines provide reliable power. The aircraft can operate over long distances. It performs effectively during military and rescue missions. Its performance contributes to its worldwide popularity.

ARMAMENT

Armed versions support combat operations. The helicopter can provide fire support when needed. Most versions focus primarily on transport missions. Its flexibility allows different armament configurations.

TECHNOLOGY

Technology upgrades keep the aircraft effective today.

DEVELOPMENT HISTORY

The B-1 program began during the 1970s. It was developed by Rockwell International. The first prototype flew in 1974. The project underwent several redesigns and improvements. The B-1B version entered service in 1986. Engineers focused on improving survivability and performance. The aircraft became a key component of US strategic forces.

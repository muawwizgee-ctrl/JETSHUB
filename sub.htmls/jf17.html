ABOUT ITS NAME

The aircraft is called the JF-17 Thunder fighter jet. The name Thunder symbolizes power, speed, and combat capability. It was jointly developed by Pakistan and China. The aircraft was designed to meet modern air combat requirements. Its name reflects its role as a frontline multirole fighter. The fighter has become a symbol of Pakistan's aviation industry. It remains one of the most important aircraft in PAF service.

OVERVIEW

The JF-17 Thunder is a lightweight multirole combat aircraft. It is designed for air-to-air and air-to-ground missions. The fighter combines affordability with modern technology. It serves as the backbone of the Pakistan Air Force fleet. The aircraft can perform a wide variety of combat roles. It is equipped with advanced avionics and weapon systems. The JF-17 continues to receive upgrades and improvements.

DEVELOPMENT HISTORY

The JF-17 was jointly developed by Pakistan and China. The project began during the 1990s to replace older fighters. Development was carried out by PAC and Chengdu Aircraft Corporation. The aircraft conducted its maiden flight in 2003. It entered operational service with the Pakistan Air Force in 2007. The fighter was designed to be affordable and effective. Its development marked a major achievement for Pakistan's aviation sector.

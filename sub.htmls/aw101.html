ABOUT ITS NAME

The AW101 is commonly known as the Merlin. The name comes from the legendary wizard Merlin. It reflects the helicopter's advanced capabilities and versatility. The Merlin name is widely recognized in military aviation. It has become a symbol of modern helicopter operations. The aircraft is respected for its reliability and performance. Today the Merlin serves in several countries worldwide.

OVERVIEW

The AW101 Merlin is a medium-lift military helicopter. It is designed for transport, search and rescue, and naval missions. The helicopter is operated by several armed forces worldwide. Its large cabin allows transportation of troops and equipment. The aircraft is known for reliability and mission flexibility. It performs effectively in land and maritime environments. The Merlin remains one of the world's leading military helicopters.

DEVELOPMENT HISTORY

The AW101 was developed by a British-Italian partnership. Development began during the 1980s. The first prototype flew in 1987. The helicopter entered service during the 1990s. It was created to meet modern military requirements. Several versions were developed for different missions. The AW101 became a major success in military aviation.

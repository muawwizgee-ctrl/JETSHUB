ABOUT ITS NAME

The aircraft is called the J-20 Mighty Dragon fighter jet. The name Dragon symbolizes strength and power in Chinese culture. It reflects the aircraft's advanced capabilities and combat role. The J-20 is China's first operational stealth fighter. Its name represents dominance and strategic importance. The aircraft is a major achievement in Chinese aviation. It serves as a symbol of modern air power development.

OVERVIEW

The J-20 is a fifth-generation stealth fighter aircraft. It is designed for air superiority and strike missions. The aircraft combines stealth, speed, and advanced technology. It is one of the most advanced fighters in Asia. The fighter can operate in contested airspace environments. It forms an important part of China's air defense strategy. The J-20 continues to receive upgrades and improvements.

DEVELOPMENT HISTORY

The J-20 was developed by Chengdu Aircraft Corporation. Its development began during the late 1990s and early 2000s. The aircraft first flew in January 2011. It entered operational service with China in 2017. The program focused on stealth and advanced combat capabilities. It marked a major milestone for China's aerospace industry. The aircraft continues to evolve with modern upgrades.

Its design emphasizes stealth, range, and survivability.

ABOUT ITS NAME

The AW139 is a medium twin-engine helicopter developed by AgustaWestland. The letters AW stand for AgustaWestland, the manufacturer. The number 139 identifies the helicopter model. It was designed for both civilian and military operations. The aircraft gained popularity due to its versatility. Its name is well known in the helicopter industry. Today the AW139 is one of the most successful helicopters in its class.

The AW139 is a modern multi-role helicopter. It is used for transport, rescue, law enforcement, and military missions. The helicopter offers high speed and excellent reliability. Its spacious cabin allows flexible mission configurations. Many governments and military forces operate the aircraft. It performs effectively in various weather conditions. The AW139 remains a popular choice worldwide.

The AW139 was developed during the late 1990s. It was created through international cooperation. The first flight took place in 2001. The helicopter entered service shortly afterward.
